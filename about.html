About Us

Our story, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Mission

At PrestigeEstates, our mission is to redefine the real estate experience by delivering unmatched service, market expertise, and a curated portfolio of South Africa's finest properties. We believe that finding a home should be a journey of excitement and confidence.

We are dedicated to understanding each client's unique needs and exceeding their expectations at every turn.

Our History

Founded in 2010, PrestigeEstates began as a boutique agency in Sandton with a vision to bring world-class real estate services to the South African market. Over the past 15 years, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in luxury property.

With over R2 billion in successful transactions, our track record speaks for itself. From modern penthouses in Cape Town to sprawling estates in Johannesburg, we have helped thousands of families find their perfect home.
